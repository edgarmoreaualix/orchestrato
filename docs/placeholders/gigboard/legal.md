## Prohibited Content
GigBoard prohibits fraudulent listings, impersonation, harassment, hate or violent content, illegal services, spam, malware links, deceptive payment requests, and IP infringement. Listings that misrepresent pay, scope, or hiring intent may be removed.

## Takedown Process
Reports may be sent to support with links, screenshots, and an issue summary. Urgent safety reports receive priority review. GigBoard may temporarily hide listings during investigation and notify affected parties where appropriate. Severe or repeated violations may lead to permanent removal.
